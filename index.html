Breaking the Ice, Building Connections

Unblock is a mobile app for iOS and Android that reinvents networking at business conferences. By leveraging AI-driven compatibility matching, it connects attendees based on shared professional and personal interests, while offering smart conversation starters to help break the ice.

Used successfully at numerous small to mid-sized conferences, Unblock has increased meaningful connections by 50% to 125% compared to previous events. Its intuitive design and personalized event integration make it a go-to tool for fostering authentic relationships and enhancing the conference experience.

Connecting Clinics and Pet Owners

We designed and built a platform that transforms how veterinary clinics engage with pet owners. The app provides a universal space for tracking pets' medical records, vaccination schedules, and appointment details, ensuring vital information is always accessible.

Beyond managing health details, the platform strengthens relationships between clinics and pet owners by enabling year-round communication. This innovative solution has improved client trust, streamlined operations, and set a new standard for personalized veterinary care.
