What is AMS

Application Maintenance and Support (AMS) is a critical aspect of the software life cycle that ensures your digital products continue to function optimally and meet user expectations. It involves a range of activities, from fixing bugs and resolving issues to updating the app to keep up with changing user needs and technological advancements. In today's digital age, where applications play a pivotal role in delivering services and engaging with customers, effective AMS is more important than ever.

The importance of AMS lies in its ability to ensure the longevity and relevance of your digital products. Regular maintenance helps improve user experience, enhance security, and keep the app updated with the latest features and technologies. According to a report by the Standish Group, approximately 70% of IT budgets are spent on maintaining and operating existing systems and software1, underscoring the significance of AMS in the software life cycle. By investing in effective AMS, businesses can ensure their digital products continue to deliver value and stay competitive in the long run.

1 Standish Group. (2022). Chaos Report. https://www.standishgroup.com/
