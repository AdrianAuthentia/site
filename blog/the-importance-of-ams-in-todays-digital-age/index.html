The Importance of AMS in Today's Digital Age

Published on
September 7, 2021
-
3 min read

In the digital age, businesses are increasingly reliant on applications to deliver their services and engage with their customers. As per a report by Statista, the number of app downloads in 2022 reached a staggering 218 billion, a clear testament to the ubiquity of apps in our daily lives1. However, the journey doesn't end with the development and launch of an app. The real challenge lies in its maintenance and support, ensuring it continues to function optimally and meet user expectations.

Application Maintenance and Support (AMS) is a critical aspect of the software life cycle. It involves not only fixing bugs and issues but also updating the app to keep up with changing user needs and technological advancements. According to a study by the Standish Group, almost 70% of IT budgets are spent on maintaining and operating existing systems and software2.

Ignoring AMS can lead to a host of problems. Outdated apps can result in poor user experience, leading to customer dissatisfaction and loss. A survey by Dimensional Research found that 80% of app users will only attempt to use a problematic app three times or fewer3. Furthermore, security vulnerabilities can expose your business to data breaches and compliance issues.

So, how can businesses effectively manage AMS? This is where Authentia Consulting comes into play. We specialize in providing top-tier AMS services, ensuring your digital products continually deliver superior user experiences. Our team of seasoned professionals brings together a wealth of experience from diverse industries, equipping us to tackle any challenge and deliver top-tier application maintenance and support.

At Authentia Consulting, we understand that every app is unique, and so are its maintenance needs. We offer a range of services, from critical bug support to minor feature enhancements, tailored to your specific requirements. Our swift response time ensures any issues are promptly addressed, minimizing downtime and disruption to your services.

In conclusion, AMS is not just about keeping your apps running—it's about ensuring they continue to add value to your business and your customers. In the fast-paced digital age, partnering with an expert like Authentia Consulting can give you the peace of mind to focus on what you do best—innovating and growing your business.
