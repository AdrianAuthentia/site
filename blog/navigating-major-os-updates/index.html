Navigating Major OS Updates

Published on
August 15, 2023
-
4 min read

In the ever-evolving landscape of mobile technology, businesses are constantly challenged to ensure their apps remain functional, user-friendly, and up-to-date. One of the most significant challenges arises when tech giants like Apple and Android roll out their annual major OS updates. These updates, while bringing a plethora of new features and improvements, can sometimes disrupt the functionality of existing apps. This is where the importance of Application Management and Support (AMS) becomes paramount, especially for in-production mobile apps.

The Risks of Ignoring Major OS Updates

Every year, a significant number of apps face issues post major OS updates. These can range from minor glitches to major functional breakdowns, leading to user frustration and potential loss of business. According to a report by Mobile Action, nearly 15% of apps experience a drop in ratings after major OS updates1. This drop is often attributed to app malfunctions that arise due to compatibility issues with the new OS.

Not staying updated can also lead to security vulnerabilities. New OS versions often come with enhanced security features. Apps not optimized for these updates can become easy targets for cyber threats. A study by Symantec revealed that outdated apps are 3 times more vulnerable to security breaches2.

How Authentia Provides a Solution

  1. International Development Teams: Authentia boasts a global presence with development teams spread across multiple time zones. This ensures that there's always a team working on potential issues, providing 24/7 coverage. No matter when an issue arises, Authentia is poised to address it promptly.
  2. Preemptive Testing: Before major OS updates are rolled out, Authentia's team conducts rigorous testing on beta versions. This proactive approach helps identify potential issues and rectify them before they impact the end-users.
  3. Continuous Monitoring: Post the OS update, our team continuously monitors app performance, ensuring any unforeseen issues are swiftly addressed.
  4. User Feedback Loop: Authentia establishes a robust feedback mechanism, allowing users to report issues directly. This ensures that real-world problems are immediately flagged and resolved.

The Authentia Advantage

By partnering with Authentia, businesses not only ensure their apps remain functional post major OS updates but also enhance user trust. A seamless app experience leads to higher user retention and positive reviews. In a digital ecosystem where 88% of users are less likely to return to an app after a poor experience3, the services Authentia offers are not just beneficial—they're essential.

In conclusion, while major OS updates by Apple and Android present challenges, they also offer opportunities for apps to leverage new features and improvements. With the right AMS partner like Authentia Consulting, businesses can navigate these updates confidently, ensuring their apps continue to deliver exceptional value to their users.
